Privacy Policy for Pine Reader

Effective Date: 6th January 2026

1. Introduction

The Pineapple Company ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Pine Reader mobile application and its associated features, including the Pine-X chat assistant. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information, particularly your uploaded documents and interactions with our AI service.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

Pine Reader is designed to maximize user privacy. We do not require, collect, or store the following:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): We do not require users to register, create accounts, or provide names, emails, addresses, or phone numbers to use the core reading and AI features.
  • IP Addresses/Usage Logs: We do not track or log specific usage patterns tied to individual devices for marketing or targeted analytics.

3. Information We Collect and How We Use It

We only collect or process data necessary to provide and improve the service.

Category Data Collected Purpose and Handling
User Documents PDF, DOCX, XLSX, TXT, etc., uploaded by the user. Local Processing & Storage: All document files and their derived structured data are stored ONLY locally on your device's private storage. We do not transmit or store your full documents on our servers.
AI Interactions (Pine-X) The text of your questions, the relevant document snippet, and the AI's response. Service Provision: This data is sent to the Gemini API via a secure connection to generate summaries, answer questions, and perform document enhancement. We do not store these prompts or interactions on our servers after the response is delivered.
Configuration Data App settings, Bookmarks, and Reading Progress. Local Storage: This data is stored only on your device to maintain your personalized reading experience.
Analytics (Aggregated) Anonymous, aggregated data on feature usage. App Improvement: Used to identify bugs, prioritize feature development, and optimize performance. This data is collected in a way that *cannot* be traced back to an individual user.

4. Third-Party Data Sharing (Gemini API)

To provide the Pine-X AI feature, we transmit data to the Google Gemini API. We use the API according to Google's policies and ensure that **user-supplied content is not used by Google to train or improve their models.**
